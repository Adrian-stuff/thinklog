About ThinkLog

A centralized hub for developer-written articles — curated, fast, and built for the curious mind.

What is ThinkLog?

ThinkLog is a centralized news source platform that aggregates articles from top engineering blogs, community platforms, and individual developer sites into one clean, fast feed. Instead of hopping between a dozen bookmarks, you get a single stream of curated content with social features like reactions, comments, search, and sharing.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Information We Collect

ThinkLog is a read-only aggregator. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties. When you sign in, we store only the authentication data provided by Supabase Auth (email, display name, and avatar URL if applicable).

How We Use Your Data

The information we collect is used solely to:

  • Authenticate you when you sign in
  • Associate your comments and reactions with your profile
  • Improve the platform based on anonymous usage patterns

Cookies & Tracking

We use essential session cookies for authentication purposes only. We do not use third-party tracking cookies, analytics trackers, or advertising cookies. Supabase Auth may set a session cookie to keep you logged in.

Data Retention

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us. Comments and reactions posted under your account will be anonymized upon account deletion.

Third-Party Services

ThinkLog uses Supabase for authentication and database hosting. Their respective privacy policies apply to the data they process on our behalf. We also display content aggregated from third-party RSS feeds — we do not host or control that content.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please reach out to the development team through the project repository.
